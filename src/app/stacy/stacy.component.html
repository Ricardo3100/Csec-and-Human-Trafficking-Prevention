Meet Stacy

Stacy is a 14 year old girl who has been in foster care with her two other siblings who are seven and nine years old respectively for the last three years.
Stacy Enjoys Reading Science fiction and is looking forward to seeing what her latest grade in English will be. If she recieves an A she will be allowed to have a meal with her sbiblings.

As the oldest sibling Stacy has taken care of her siblings while in foster care but she is still far from a typical teenage girl moving from home to home as often as she does. Join stacy as she goes about her day and see what decisions she makes as we learn about human trafficking along the way.
